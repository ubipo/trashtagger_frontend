About Us

We are a team of six people from different countries in Europe and we joined together to make a project for the Hackathon event. After working together for 24 h #trashtag platform was created.

A platform to mark polluted spots and organise cleanup-events, to get points for recycling and . compete with friends. More than just a game - our chance to make a change!

Joining this movement, you can do both - help the Planet and have fun!
